Donate Life Arizona

I am a proud advocate for organ donation and transplantation, sharing my story to raise awareness and encourage others to register as organ donors.

In September 2021, I was featured by Donate Life Arizona in a statewide campaign on the impact of organ, eye, and tissue donation. As a liver transplant recipient, I shared my story to encourage students and young adults to register as organ donors and to raise awareness about the importance of transplantation in saving lives.

This initiative was part of the Campus Challenge in partnership with Arizona colleges and universities.
